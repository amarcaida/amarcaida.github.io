BATTLE is a completely RNG-based game where you control three characters: [BELLA], [ROK], and [MISS NO] and fight three entities.

Click on the Game Title in the Start Menu to see Tips and Tricks.

TERMS USED:

HP (HEALTH POINTS)

Each character you play starts off with 10 HP. This is basically their health. Once a character’s HP turns 0, you will no longer be able to heal them nor use their skills and abilities, so be sure to pay close attention!

MANA

These are used to activate a character’s SPECIAL ABILITY. You get 2 mana with every turn.

NORMAL SKILL

Each character has a unique skill that can be used at any given time.

Bella: [HEAL] – Let’s you heal one team member by choice.

Rok: [MERCY] – Let’s you run away and spare your enemies at a normal fight.

Miss No: [ATTACK] – Let’s you deal damage to one enemy by choice.

SPECIAL ABILITY

Each character has a special ability that can only be activated when you have a certain amount of mana.

Bella: [HP BURST] – Heals the whole team. Requires 3 Mana.

Rok: [BLOCK] – Blocks out the enemy’s attack and deals damage to the main boss. Requires 2 Mana.

Miss No: [MASS MURDER] – Deals damage to all enemies. Requires 5 Mana.

WIN/LOSE CONDITIONS

To win, simply deal enough damage for the enemies’ HPs to turn 0. Do not worry though, they’re just having a really long nap.

The game brings you back to the starting menu upon winning/losing.
