You found the tips page! Below are some tips to successfully defeat the big man boss

Tip #1: Start off with Miss No’s Normal Skill and spam the hecc out of it.

Tip #2: Pay attention to your characters’ HP.

Tip #3: Never and I mean NEVER use Rok’s Normal Skill. It’s there solely to waste players’ turns (not only do you not deal any damage but you also don’t gain mana).

Tip #4: Special Abilities have no chances of failure, so It’s better to use Bella’s HP BURST instead of her HEAL whenever you have enough mana for it.

Tip $5: Have fun!
